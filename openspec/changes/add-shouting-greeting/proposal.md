# add-shouting-greeting

## Why

The demo needs a third safe change that is visually obvious in output and can
run after localization support.

## What Changes

- Add a shout option that uppercases the final greeting.
- Support shouting for both English and Spanish greetings.
- Add unit coverage for shout behavior.

## Non-Goals

- No full command-line parser.
- No punctuation customization.
- No remote push or archive until OpenSpec Shipper safety flags are enabled.
